Zmanim Alarm

Privacy Policy — Last updated: July 2026

Summary: Zmanim Alarm does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Everything stays on your device.

1. Information We Collect

Zmanim Alarm does not collect any personal information. The app operates entirely offline and does not communicate with any external servers.

The following data is stored locally on your device only:

  • Your GPS coordinates or manually entered location (used solely to calculate Jewish prayer times)
  • Your alarm configurations (name, time offset, ringtone, days)
  • App preferences (language, dark mode, calculation method)

2. Location Data

Location access is used exclusively to calculate accurate Zmanim (Jewish prayer times) for your geographic position. Location data is:

  • Never transmitted to any server
  • Never shared with third parties
  • Stored locally only if you choose to save your position
  • Optional — you can enter coordinates manually without enabling GPS

3. Permissions Used

  • Location — to determine your position for Zmanim calculation
  • Notifications — to display alarm notifications
  • Exact alarms — to trigger alarms at the precise Zman time
  • Battery optimization exemption — to ensure alarms fire even when the phone is idle
  • Boot completed — to reschedule alarms after a device restart

4. Third-Party Services

Zmanim Alarm does not use any analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or any other third-party service. There are no SDKs that collect data.

5. Data Retention

All data is stored locally on your device. Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all associated data.

6. Children's Privacy

Zmanim Alarm does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new date.
